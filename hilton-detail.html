Game Hilton In Tropical

Game Hilton in Tropical is the second installment in the Hilton adventure series. Unlike the previous series, which focused on Indonesian history and culture, Hilton in Tropical is set in a tropical forest that reflects Indonesia's climate. The game also features many other exciting mini-games.
