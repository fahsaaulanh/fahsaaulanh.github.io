Game Timescape2

TIMESCAPE2 is a 3D multiplayer puzzle-platformer game for two players that tests players' collaboration, teamwork and problem solving skills in facing obstacles and solving puzzles using the ability to move in time, in this case the future and the past which are 1 century apart. With the unique switch time mechanic, players can move between the past and the future to find the required items or change the circumstances and conditions of the map to complete puzzles at each level.
