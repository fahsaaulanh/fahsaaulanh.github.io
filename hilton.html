Game Overview

Hilton in Tropical is the second installment in the Hilton adventure series. This chapter shifts the focus from Indonesian history to the nation's vibrant climate, setting the adventure within a lush tropical forest.

Key Features:

  • New Setting: Explores a beautiful and challenging tropical forest environment.
  • Adventure Series: Continues the story of the protagonist, Hilton.
  • Mini-Games: Features many new and exciting mini-games to enhance the player experience.
