Project Overview

Colorfun is a commercial project developed in collaboration with Signature Gudang Garam for their event activations. This interactive game has been successfully deployed and featured at numerous concerts and events held by vendors partnering with Signature.

I am proud to have developed and launched the Colorfun application, which has been used by thousands of participants, significantly enhancing engagement and entertainment value during the events.

Key Highlights:

  • Commercial Collaboration: A successful partnership with a major brand, Signature Gudang Garam.
  • Large-Scale Deployment: Engaged thousands of users at live concerts and marketing events.
  • Brand Activation: Boosted brand interaction and entertainment through interactive gameplay.
