App Overview

As a language enthusiast, I've always been fascinated by a simple fact: we can remember song lyrics from decades ago, yet struggle with vocabulary from a textbook. I decided to build a solution to this problem myself, and the result is Lingo Song.

Lingo Song is an interactive English learning app that harnesses the power of music to make learning feel less like a chore and more like a jam session. It transforms passive listening into an active learning experience.
