Math Falls

Math Falls is a project for the Interactive Multimedia Programming course, designed as a 3D platformer educational game. Players must reach the finish line before time runs out, navigating obstacles and solving math problems along the way. As levels increase, the challenges and randomly generated math questions become more difficult. Players who achieve the highest scores will have their names displayed on the leaderboard. *Only play on pc or laptop
